# AgriVision Demo Script (5-7 Minutes)

## 0) Hook (20s)
- "Farmers lose money due to delayed disease action and poor selling timing."
- "AgriVision gives one decision console for disease, market, irrigation, and planning."

## 1) Login + Profile (60s)
- Open app and login with mobile number.
- Go to Profile and Planner tab.
- Save farmer details and current crop.
- Judge takeaway: Personalization and persistent records.

## 2) Dashboard + WOW Feature (90s)
- Show weather and soil cards.
- Open Auto-Irrigation Copilot.
- Move stress and rain sliders.
- Explain decision updates in real time:
  - Irrigate now vs delay
  - Liters/acre
  - Fuel savings
  - Yield lift
- Judge takeaway: Actionable AI automation, not static analytics.

## 3) Disease Detection (90s)
- Pick crop + upload image placeholder.
- Run AI scan.
- Show confidence, severity, treatment, prevention.
- Then open Profile and Planner and load disease history.
- Judge takeaway: Full lifecycle from diagnosis to tracking.

## 4) Market Intelligence (60s)
- Search crop (e.g., rice).
- Show market vs MSP gap and trend.
- Explain sell timing support.
- Judge takeaway: Direct economic value.

## 5) Season Plan Generation (60s)
- Generate season plan from profile.
- Show estimated cost, revenue, and projected profit.
- Show timeline activities.
- Judge takeaway: Decision support from sowing to selling.

## 6) Close (30s)
- "In one platform, farmer gets crop health + irrigation + price + economics."
- "Built as low-cost deployable MVP with clear path to ML + IoT scale."
